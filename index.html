Visualizing the Customer Journey

This interactive deck allows you to explore the entire customer journey, and help identify paths that are most associated with high LTV.


How To Use This Tool

Hover your cursor over any path/node to display the average LTV of that path, how many users fall under this particular path, and what percentage of your entire user base it represents.
